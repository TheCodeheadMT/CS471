Understanding Bayesian Networks

A Bayesian Network tracks cause-and-effect relationships. Changing a node at the bottom (an effect) allows the network to perform inference to guess the state of the nodes at the top (the causes).

Joint Probability Definition:
$P(T, D, P) = P(T) \cdot P(D) \cdot P(P|T, D)$
